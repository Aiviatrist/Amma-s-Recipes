Panner Butter Masala


Ingredients:

. 200g panner

. 2 tomatoes

. 1 onion

. 1 tbs ginger/garlic paste

. 1 tbs garam masala

. 1 chilli pepper

. 2 tsp chilli powder

. 1/2 tsp tumeric

. 1/2 cup heavy cream

. 50g butter


Recipe:

1. Cut the panner into small cubes and fry in butter

2. Remove the panner from the pan and saute cut onion, tomato and chilli pepper

3. Make a puree of onion, tomato, ginger/garlic paste and other dry masala

4. Melt the remaining butter and cook the puree until the raw taste disappears

5. Once the puree is cooked add the fried panner and chilli pepper to the puree and cook well

6. Finish the dish with adding heavy cream

7. Serve it with basmati rice and enjoy!


Vegetarian/Vegan Samosa


Ingredients:

. 1 cup Wheat flour

. 2 tsp Salt(to taste)

. 2 cups boiled potato

. 1 cup boiled green peas

. 1 cup water

. 2 green chillies

. 1 tbs garam masala


Recipe:

1. Make tiny Chapatti like flat sheets

2. Let it dry for 2 mins

3. Mix in a bowl green chilli, potato and peas with garam masala added to it (this will be used as stuffing)

4. Make a cone shape out of the sheets and fill it with the stuffing

5. Cover the top cone sealing the edges with water

6. Rest the samosa with the cone upwards until all samosas have stuffings in them

7. Fry each samosa in oil at low to medium flame until the coat is crispy

8. Serve it with mint chutney and enjoy!


Chilli Bajji


Ingredients:

. 6 banana peppers

. 1/2 cup gram flour

. 1/4 rice flour

. 1 tbs corn flour

. 2 tsp chilli powder

. 1 tsp salt

. water (as required)

. 2 cup oil


Recipe:

1. Slit the peppers and remove the seeds within

2. In a bowl add all the dry ingredients and mix it with water until it forms a thick batter that can coat the peppers

3. Heat the oil

4. Dip the peppers into the batter until it is coated all over and fry then in the oil one by one making sure it is cooked throughout

5.Serve it with coconut chutney and enjoy!
